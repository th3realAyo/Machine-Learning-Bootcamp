TYPES OF MACHINE LEARNING (ALGORITHMS):
- SUPERVISED LEARNING
- UNSUPERVISED LEARNING

SUPERVISED LEARNING: These ML algorithms are trained on labeled datasets - meaning each input has a known output. i.e Email spam filters, House price prediction, Image recognition.

TYPES OF SUPERVISED LEARNING:
- Classification: Used when the output is a category or label. Male/Female, Spam/Not Spam, Accept/Reject
- Regression: Used when the output is a quantity or continuous numerical value. i.e Temperature, House Price, Height, Salary.

Classification: Predict category/groups
Regression: Predict number

Classification Algorithm:
1) Logistic Regression
2) Decision Tree: Divides output into different branches of decision
3) Random Forest: Combines multiple/numeroud decision trees
4) SVM - Support Vector Machine
5) KNN - K Nearest Neigbour: Based on nearest datapoins
6) Naive Bayes: Based on probability

Regression Algorithm:
1) Linear Regression
2) Decision Tree RegX
3) Random Forest RegX
4) SVM - Support Vector Machine Regression
5) Polynomial RegX
6) Ridge & Lasso RegX

UNSUPERVISED LEARNING:
Unsupervised leaening deals with unlabelled data. The system tries to identitfy hidden pastterns in the data without knowing the correct answer. i.e Customer Segmentation(Group customers based on similar purchase pattern), Market Basket Analysis (Product frequenetly bought together), Gene Clustering in Biology.

Association: Discovering intresting relationships or rules among variables. i.e People who buy bread often buy butter.
